Our Work Process

1

Timber Receiving

Raw rubber wood logs are received and unloaded at our facility with proper inspection and handling.

2

Wood Preparation

Logs are selected and prepared as high-quality raw material for the next stage of production.

3

Drying & Treatment

Wood is kiln-dried and boron treated to reduce moisture and improve durability and pest resistance.

4

Conditioning & Sorting

Dried planks are conditioned to optimal moisture levels and sorted to maintain consistent quality.

5

Cutting & Finishing

Wood is shaped to the required dimensions and finished through sanding for a smooth surface.

6

Packing & Export

Final products are securely packed and loaded for safe transportation to international markets.

About

About TLK Export

TLK Export is a Sri Lankan manufacturer and exporter specializing in wooden brush handles and other wooden products designed for international markets.

Crafting Excellence Through Tradition and Innovation

We are dedicated artisans and export professionals committed to bringing the finest Sri Lankan wooden crafts to global markets with uncompromising quality and service.

Our production process combines traditional woodworking skills with modern manufacturing methods to ensure durability, consistent dimensions, and export-grade finishing. Every product is carefully crafted using responsibly sourced timber and inspected under strict quality standards before shipment.

Over the years, TLK Export has built strong relationships with international buyers by maintaining reliable production capacity, transparent communication, and dependable delivery schedules.

About TLK Export

Leading the wooden craft export industry with authentic Sri Lankan artistry and exceptional quality standards that meet international market demands

Crafting Excellence Through Tradition and Innovation

We are dedicated artisans and export professionals committed to bringing the finest Sri Lankan wooden crafts to global markets with uncompromising quality and service.

TLK Export specializes in handcrafted wooden products that showcase the rich heritage of Sri Lankan craftsmanship. Each piece is carefully crafted by skilled artisans using sustainably sourced timber, ensuring authenticity and environmental responsibility in every export shipment.

Our commitment to excellence extends from the workshop to international delivery. We maintain rigorous quality control standards, handle all export documentation, and ensure timely shipments to retailers, wholesalers, and importers across the globe.
